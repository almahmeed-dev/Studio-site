FodmapReintro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

FodmapReintro is developed by MubarakLab. The short version: your symptom data never leaves your device. We don't collect it, can't see it, and don't want it.

Data collection

Everything you enter in FodmapReintro — your challenge plan, daily check-ins, symptom scores, notes, and trigger profile — is stored locally on your device only. There is no account, no sign-up, and no server of ours that receives your health data. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through your Apple ID. To validate your subscription we use RevenueCat, which receives your purchase receipt and a random anonymous identifier generated for your app install. This identifier is not linked to your name, email, or identity, and no health data is ever sent with it.

Notifications

Daily reminders are local notifications scheduled on your device. They are optional and never pass through our servers.

Third-party services

Other than RevenueCat (purchase validation only), FodmapReintro uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA and similar)

Because your data stays on your device, access, correction, and deletion are in your hands: edit or delete anything in the app, or delete the app to remove everything. For the anonymous purchase record held by RevenueCat, email us with your approximate purchase date and we'll request its deletion within 30 days.

Children

FodmapReintro is not directed at children under 16 and we do not knowingly process children's data.

Health disclaimer

FodmapReintro is a planning and tracking tool, not medical advice. Use it alongside your dietitian or doctor.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
